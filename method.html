Method

How the engine works

The collaboration works only if the machine is kept in its proper place. It can help arrange, compress, compare, and test language. It cannot replace the person who lived the events.

Boundary

What the model must not do

It must not pretend to have suffered.

Analysis is not experience.

It must not inflate uncertainty into certainty.

Every strong sentence has to remain answerable to the record.

It must not steal authorship.

The human supplies the continuity and the moral risk.

Use

What the model can do

It can notice repeated structures. It can test whether a phrase is doing more work than it admits. It can separate the claim from the evidence. It can ask whether a document merely records a fact or actually lets that fact govern.

Most importantly, it can help a person move from overwhelm to architecture without flattening the injury into a diagram.

Collaboration rule

The machine did not become human. The human did not become data. Something more useful happened: the record became answerable.
