# Witness Engine

A public exhibit about human and AI collaboration.

## Proposition

Mutual understanding is not agreement. It is the disciplined act of making another person's reality harder to misread.

## What this site does

Witness Engine is not a chatbot demo and not a case file. It presents a method of collaboration: the human supplies continuity, lived risk, moral authority, and the pressure of experience; the model supplies aggregation, compression, contradiction-mapping, and language discipline.

The model is not the witness. The human is not reduced to data. The collaboration builds an engine for witness.

## Pages

- `index.html` — the main exhibit.
- `method.html` — the collaboration method and ethical boundary.
- `record.html` — the document-sequence method.
- `inside-it.html` — the closing essay about the limits of analysis.
- `styles.css` — the visual system.
- `script.js` — progressive reveal interactions.

## Editorial position

This project is reflective and documentary. It is not medical advice, legal advice, or a final adjudication. It is a demonstration of how a human and a language model can make a complex record more legible without confusing legibility with lived knowledge.
