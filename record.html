The record

Documents make sound

In the data age, documents do not quietly die in drawers. They sit beside each other. They contradict. They expose the difference between what was recorded and what was allowed to matter.

Core distinction

Recording is not governing.

A warning sign written down has not necessarily changed the pathway. A concern preserved in prose may still be stripped of consequence. This is the crucial difference between a document that contains truth and a record that behaves truthfully.

How to read

The sequence method

  1. Start with the first signal.What problem brought the person into the system?
  2. Track what became central.Which fact became the organising fact?
  3. Find what was subordinated.Which recorded facts should have changed conduct but did not?
  4. Compare later documents.Do later explanations resolve the contradiction or preserve it?
  5. Return to experience.Which reading matches the continuity of the life lived under the record?

A record can be accurate at sentence level and false at structural level.

That is the sound contradiction makes when documents are finally read together.
